Privacy Policy for Knitting App

Last updated: December 2025

Knitting (“the App”) is a standalone iOS application that allows users to create, edit, and manage knitting patterns. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and what rights you have.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The Knitting App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not track user behavior, do not use analytics tools, and do not have any external servers.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All projects, settings, images, PDF exports, and knitting patterns you create are stored locally on your device only. We never send your data anywhere.

3. No Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • advertising networks
  • analytics frameworks
  • third-party SDKs
  • external databases or servers

4. In-App Purchases

The App may offer paid features through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Apple may collect purchase-related information according to Apple's Privacy Policy. The Knitting App does not see or store your payment data.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect any data from users, including children. It is safe for all age groups.

6. Your Rights

You have full control of your data because all information is stored only on your device. To remove all data, simply delete the app from your device.
