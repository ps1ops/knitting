Privacy Policy for Knitting

Last updated: December 2025

Knitting (“the App”) is a standalone iOS application that allows users to create and manage knitting patterns. We respect your privacy and do not collect any personal data.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal information or usage data.

2. Local Storage

All projects, settings, and files are stored directly on your device and never transferred to external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, tracking tools, or databases.

4. In-App Purchases

The App may offer optional paid features through Apple’s in-app purchase system. Apple may collect payment-related data, handled according to Apple’s Privacy Policy.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect any data from children.
