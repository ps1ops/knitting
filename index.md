# KnitCraft — Privacy Policy
**Last updated:** November 2025

KnitCraft (“the App”) respects your privacy and does not collect, store, or share any personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles data and what information is stored on your device.

## 1. Information We Collect
KnitCraft does not collect any personally identifiable information.

All data is stored locally on your device, including:
- knitting projects and patterns  
- project names  
- user preferences (such as grid size and symbol settings)

The App does not connect to external servers and does not transmit any information over the internet.

## 2. Data Storage and Security
All information is stored securely on the user’s device using system storage technologies (Core Data and the File System).

Deleting the App permanently removes all associated data.

We do not have access to any user data at any time.

## 3. Third-Party Services
KnitCraft does not use any third-party services, including:
- analytics platforms  
- advertising networks  
- tracking tools  
- social media integrations  

No information is shared with any external service.

## 4. Permissions
The App may request optional access to Photos for the following purposes:
- exporting a pattern as an image  
- importing an image into a project  

KnitCraft only accesses Photos after explicit user permission.  
No images are uploaded or transmitted.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
KnitCraft is safe for users of all ages.  
The App does not collect personal information from children or adults and contains no ads, tracking, or external links.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
The latest version will always be available on this page.
