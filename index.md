# Knitting — Privacy Policy
**Last updated:** November 2025

Knitting (“the App”) respects your privacy and does not collect, store, or share any personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles data and what information is stored on your device.

## 1. Information We Collect
Knitting does not collect any personally identifiable information.

All data is stored locally on your device, including:
- knitting projects and patterns  
- project names  
- user preferences (such as grid size and symbol settings)

The App does not connect to external servers and does not transmit information over the internet.

## 2. Data Storage and Security
All information is stored securely on the user’s device using Core Data and the File System.

Deleting the App permanently removes all stored data.

We do not have access to any user data.

## 3. Third-Party Services
Knitting does not use any third-party services, including:
- analytics  
- advertising  
- tracking  
- social media integrations  

No data is shared with external services.

## 4. Permissions
The App may request optional access to Photos for:
- exporting a knitting pattern as an image  
- importing an image into a project  

Knitting only accesses Photos with explicit user permission and never uploads or transmits images.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
Knitting is safe for all ages.  
The App does not collect personal data and contains no ads, tracking, or external links.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.  
The latest version will always be available on this page.
